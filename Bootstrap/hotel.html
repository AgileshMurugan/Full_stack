About us

Learn more about us

Our Tasty-Treat restaurant is an establishment that prepares and serves food and drinks to customers.
Meals are generally served and eaten on the premises, but our restaurants also offer take-out and food delivery services.
Restaurants vary greatly in appearance and offerings, including a wide variety of cuisines and service models ranging from inexpensive fast-food restaurants and cafeterias
to mid-priced family restaurants, to high-priced luxury establishments.
